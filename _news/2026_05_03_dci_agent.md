[Beyond Semantic Similarity (DCI-Agent)](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.05242) is out, rethinking retrieval for agentic search through direct corpus interaction. It was ranked [\#1 Paper of the Day on Hugging Face Papers](https://huggingface.co/papers/2605.05242). The [code](https://github.com/DCI-Agent/DCI-Agent-Lite) and [dataset](https://huggingface.co/datasets/DongfuJiang/AgenticSearchQA) are also available.
